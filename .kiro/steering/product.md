# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
An interactive data visualization and analysis platform that showcases China's historic $1.2 trillion trade surplus in 2025. The platform demonstrates how China achieved record-breaking export performance despite intense US-China trade tensions, rising tariffs, and global economic pressures. This showcase transforms complex economic data into compelling visual narratives that reveal the structural drivers behind this unprecedented trade phenomenon.

## Target Users
**Primary Audience:**
- **Economics Researchers & Analysts**: Need comprehensive data analysis and insights into trade patterns, structural economic shifts, and policy impacts
- **Policy Makers & Government Officials**: Require clear understanding of trade dynamics, geopolitical implications, and strategic economic trends
- **Business Leaders & Investors**: Seek insights into global supply chain shifts, market opportunities, and economic risk factors
- **Academics & Students**: Want educational resources for understanding international trade, economic policy, and China's role in global commerce
- **Journalists & Media**: Need accessible, visually compelling data to support economic reporting and analysis

**User Needs:**
- Clear visualization of complex trade data and economic relationships
- Interactive exploration of different factors driving the trade surplus
- Historical context and comparative analysis
- Accessible explanations of economic concepts and implications
- Shareable insights and data visualizations

## Key Features
**Core Visualization Features:**
- **Interactive Trade Flow Maps**: 3D globe showing export/import flows between China and global regions
- **Dynamic Timeline Analysis**: Animated charts showing trade surplus evolution from 2010-2025
- **Sector Deep Dives**: Detailed analysis of "New Three" exports (EVs, batteries, solar panels) vs traditional manufacturing
- **Geographic Pivot Visualization**: Interactive maps showing China's shift from US markets to Global South
- **Comparative Analysis Tools**: Side-by-side comparisons with historical trade powers (Japan 1990s, Germany 2010s)

**Data Analysis Features:**
- **Structural Driver Analysis**: Interactive breakdown of the seven key factors behind the surplus
- **Currency & Deflation Impact**: Real-time visualization of exchange rate and price effects
- **Import Substitution Tracker**: Progress visualization of China's "Made in China 2025" goals
- **Tariff Impact Assessment**: Before/after analysis of trade war effects

**Educational Features:**
- **Guided Story Mode**: Step-by-step narrative explaining the economic phenomenon
- **Glossary & Explainers**: Interactive definitions of economic terms and concepts
- **Research Integration**: Direct access to source documents and detailed analysis
- **Export Functionality**: Generate reports, charts, and data exports for further analysis

## Business Objectives
**Primary Goals:**
- **Educational Impact**: Make complex international trade dynamics accessible to diverse audiences
- **Research Contribution**: Provide a comprehensive, interactive resource for economic analysis
- **Public Understanding**: Increase awareness of China's economic transformation and global trade implications
- **Academic Value**: Support research and teaching in international economics and policy studies

**Success Metrics:**
- User engagement time and interaction depth
- Educational content completion rates
- Data visualization sharing and citation
- Academic and media references to the platform
- User feedback on clarity and usefulness of insights

## User Journey
**Discovery Phase:**
1. Users arrive via academic references, social media, or search engines
2. Landing page presents the "$1.2 Trillion Paradox" with compelling headline statistics
3. Interactive preview showcases key visualizations and findings

**Exploration Phase:**
1. **Guided Tour**: New users can take a structured journey through the key findings
2. **Free Exploration**: Advanced users can dive directly into specific data visualizations
3. **Deep Dive Analysis**: Researchers can access detailed sector and regional breakdowns
4. **Historical Context**: Users can explore comparative data and historical precedents

**Analysis Phase:**
1. **Interactive Tools**: Users manipulate variables to understand cause-and-effect relationships
2. **Custom Views**: Create personalized dashboards focusing on specific aspects
3. **Data Export**: Download charts, data, and analysis for external use
4. **Sharing**: Generate shareable insights and visualizations for presentations or reports

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understanding of the structural factors behind China's trade surplus
- Insight into global supply chain dynamics and economic policy impacts
- Appreciation for the complexity of international trade relationships
- Knowledge of China's economic transformation and global positioning
